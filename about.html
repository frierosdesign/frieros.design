Work About me

Hi! I’m Anabel

I'm a Barcelona-based experience designer and editor looking to further my career in the design space.

I'm passionate about solving problems in creative ways, improving the lives of others through intuitive designs and am constantly looking to learn new things everyday.

After completing a Master in UX/UI Design at Nuclio Digital School, I have continued teaching myself about product design through attending workshops, reading books and blogs and applying this knowledge on several projects.

I love to craft innovative digital products that have a positive impact on individuals and society. As a UX/UI designer, I focus on human-centered designs but always keeping in mind the company's goals. That’s why I value the importance of UX strategy for business growth.

I enjoy refining and improving existing products, but also building new services and end-to-end products. I’m comfortable working on all aspects of the product cycle, from ideation and research to prototyping journeys, as well as crafting UIs, testing and iterating.

Think Big. Start Small. Learn Fast. Repeat.

If you think I would be a good fit to your design team, feel free to contact me and let’s talk!

I’m always looking to collaborate on interesting and innovative projects. If you are a fellow designer, let’s connect and work on projects together.
