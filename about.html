A little bit about myself

I'm a UX/UI Designer based in Barcelona with experience in delivering end-to-end UX/UI design for software products. I'm passionate about solving problems, improving the lives of others through design and am constantly looking to learn new things everyday.

After completing a Master in UX/UI Design at Nuclio Digital School, I have continued applying my skills on several projects and teaching myself about product design through attending workshops, reading books and blogs and applying this knowledge in my spare time. I enjoy redesigning products as well as working on end-to-end products, from ideation to development.
